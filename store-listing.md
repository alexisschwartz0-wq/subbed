## Full description (4000 char max)

Subbed connects yoga, Pilates, sound bath, and fitness instructors with studio owners — for last-minute subs and long-term hiring, all in one place.

**For instructors**
Browse open listings by discipline, type, and location. Apply in one click, or send a quick message to ask a question — no cold outreach required. Your profile stays private until you apply or reach out, so you're never browsable without your consent.

- Get discovered by studios looking for exactly what you teach
- Showcase your disciplines, certifications, and availability
- Pick up last-minute subs or land part-time and full-time roles
- Message studios directly to lock in the details

**For studios**
Post an opening in minutes — last-minute sub, part-time, or full-time — and see who's interested. No browsing through instructors who haven't opted in; you only see the profile of someone who's actually applied or messaged you about your listing.

- Post permanent and part-time positions, or fill an emergency sub spot fast
- Find qualified instructors instantly, with certifications and reviews included
- Build a reliable roster over time
- One platform for subs and full hires

**Message directly, no middleman**
Once you find the right fit, chat right in Subbed to nail down the details — no phone tag, no email chains, no third-party scheduling app.

Subbed is starting in Orange County, built by people who've spent decades in the fitness and wellness industry as instructors and studio owners themselves — and built to grow far beyond it.
